Preventive Maintenance

With today's complex HVAC systems, it is essential to have routine maintenance performed by trained technicians who understand how to keep these systems working at their best. When your HVAC systems are running at peak efficiency they demand less energy to keep you comfortable. This results in savings on monthly energy costs. Properly maintained equipment is less likely to fail when you need it most.

Poorly maintained systems have a shorter life expectancy of one that is inspected and maintained on a regular basis. Minor problems undetected can lead to major repair bills. Midwest Mechanical Services, offers maintenance agreements tailor made for every customer's individual needs.

Benefits of Preventive Maintenance Include

  • Fewer costly repairs
  • Insure year-round performance
  • Guaranteed energy savings
  • 10% off any material used
  • $5.00/hr off normal commercial labor rates
  • Maintain permanent service records
  • Receive priority service on emergency calls
